# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

Yogesh Patil Portfolio Website

## Overview

A modern and professional portfolio website designed to showcase Yogesh Patil's skills, projects, coding achievements, education, and contact information. The portfolio will be used for internship applications, placements, and professional networking.

---

## Objectives

### Primary Goals

* Showcase technical skills and projects.
* Demonstrate problem-solving ability through coding achievements.
* Provide recruiters with an easy way to evaluate experience.
* Increase internship and placement opportunities.
* Establish a professional online presence.

### Success Criteria

* Responsive across all devices.
* Fast page load speed.
* Professional UI/UX.
* Easy navigation.
* Deployed and publicly accessible.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Users

* Recruiters
* Hiring Managers
* Placement Coordinators
* Internship Providers

### Secondary Users

* Fellow Developers
* Open Source Contributors
* Professional Network Connections
